Welcome to Eldershare

Elderhomeshare provides peace of mind for elderly citizens and their families by providing a way for them to live happily and independently at home. Home owners are matched up with vetted home-share companions, who move in at an affordable cost and help out around the house. This is a cost saving alternative care solution to traditional live in care models.

How it works?

Homeowners work with us to identify their desired characteristics in a companion by completing the needs assessment. Then, they may post a listing and contact the interested companions who they believe would be best suited for their home. After the potential companions are narrowed down to the final choice, Elderhomeshare will set up the match and you will have a one-month trial period to see if this is, indeed, a good match for you.

Homeshare companions fill out an online profile and contact homeowners whom they are interested in. Once the companions has matched with a homeowner, they are vetted and then will move in to the home for a one-month trial period to see if this is a good match for you.

Fees For Homeowners

The annual fee for the homeowner is 950 euro. This includes a one-to-one consultation in your home, finding and placing a home share companion, providing a good fit for your needs.

Fees For Companions

The placement fee to be paid to Elderhomeshare is 450 euro. The monthly contribution in exchange for quality time and help at home (average 8 no more than 10 hours a week) is usually 150 euro per month with no other bills.
