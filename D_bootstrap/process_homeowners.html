The Process

1. Create an account

Create an account by filling out the application, attached here. This provides us with information about you to ensure your match fits your needs. In order to create the account, you will need to pay a 250 euro down payment.

2. Post your listing

You may post a listing with basic information– your city, your gender, your age– and wait for companions to message you. This way, you can see who is interested rather than reaching out first.

3. View profiles of interested home share companions

Login and view information for your potential companion! Choose who you would like to reach out to, and we will provide you with contact information.

4. Meet the potential companion

Elderhomeshare encourages you to meet with your companion before officially asking them to move in. For security purposes, Elderhomeshare will vet them before you meet up, and we recommend meeting outside of the house for the first time.

5. Pick your companion

After you pick your final choice for a home share companion, Elderhomeshare gives you the option to have your companion Garda Vetted.

6. Sign the homeshare agreement

Elderhomeshare writes up a customized home share agreement that both you and the companion sign off on, based off of the needs assessment from the beginning of this process. After you sign, you pay the rest of the fee.

7. Move in!

After your companion has been vetted, they will move in for a 1-month trial period where we periodically will check in. If both parties are happy with the match, then you can continue with the current living situation!
