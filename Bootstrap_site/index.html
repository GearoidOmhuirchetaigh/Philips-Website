Welcome to Elderhomeshare

 

Elderhomeshare provides peace of mind for elderly citizens to live happily and independently at home by matching up home share companions. It is an alternative home support solution for the elderly and can be work well instead of paying for live in care at night, if the homeowner does not need night time assistance. For elderly people who would like to continue to live with a good level of personal independence in their own home , this exchange offers the homeowner security, companionship and someone to help out for an agreed amount of time each week.
Elderhomeshare makes up a tailored home share agreement to meet the needs of the home owner. Additionally, we carry out a needs assessment. We identify the requirements of the elderly person and create a successful home share match. For the home share companion, it's a great way to live an affordable life style in exchange for offering support and friendship.
For the elderly person, who is living alone and their concerned adult children, it is proving to be a rewarding way to solve the problem of care in the home. Home share companions provide support to the elderly person. For example, by spending quality time together, keeping the house tidy, and going to the shop. It is not a solution to personal care but can reduce the need and expense of traditional care services. All home share companions are vetted.
If you are looking for a live in solution for an elderly parent this may be a suitable option, we are always happy to talk on the phone to discuss your needs.
